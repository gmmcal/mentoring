Improve your development skills with 1-1 mentoring

Hello! My name is Gustavo Cunha, I have almost 20 years of experience developing all sorts of web-based software. I strongly believe that teaching is the best way to learn, so I want to dedicate some of my time to teach something to you and, in that experience exchange, also learn from you.

Expectations

In order for everything work well and we can learn together, we need to setup some ground rules on what I can offer and you can expect from me.

  1. There is no such thing as a stupid question. Feel free to ask anything and I'll try my best to help you.
  2. I will not pair program with you. The idea is to talk about development carreer, not write code along with you. Pair programming takes much more time and requires previous domain knowledge that I might not have enough.
  3. I can try to do code review with you. For that, please send a link for your repository/(pull|merge) request during your appointment and be clear that your want a code-review session.
  4. I will not review code that is intended to be submitted for a job application. This is not fair to other applicants. If I suspect this is the intention, I will stop the session.
  5. I can help you prepare for interviews. I've been doing this for companies already. But that is no guarantee that you will succeed your job application (but I do hope you do).
  6. I can review your CV and give you tips on how to improve. Please, send a PDF link of your CV and we can talk about it.
  7. Don't expect I know everything. I'm here to help you, but also to learn from you.

Commitment Fee

I've organized a few events on my life, and one thing I learned is that anything that is given for free lacks commitment. To avoid this issue, I decided to place what I call a commitment fee.

Application period

When applying for a session, please book with 3 business days in advance. This will give you and me enough time to process payment, check your personal needs and have all details ready for our session.

The Amount

The amount I charge for this is €10,00 (ten euros). This amount covers one session of 30 minutes.

Refund policy

  • I will not process refund for no-shows.
  • If you can't attend a session, please notify with at least 24 hours before your session. I will allow you to book a new time free of charge.
  • You are allowed to postpone 1 time per session payment. If you rebook one session and can't make it, I will not rebook nor refund.
  • If I need to postpone the session, you will be allowed to rebook two sessions instead of 1, with no extra costs.
  • You will probably receive an email from Calendly after your booking. Please, do not use the cancel nor reschedule links provided on the email without my express approval.
  • Any changes for this refund policy will be effective on the day the code is published on Github. If your booking was done before that, you will not be eligible for it, regardless if you benefit or not by the change.

Tech minority commitment

If you belong to any group that is part of a tech minority, like LGBTQA+, woman or people of color, your fee will be refunded after the session, if you want, according to the following criteria:

  • I have 3 business days to confirm the refund.
  • I will try to refund you in full minus Wise Transfer Fees. I will send you a proof of the transfer via email.
  • Whenever possible, I will try to absorve the fees and return 100% of the Commitment Fee. There is no guarantee that this will happen, unfortunately.
  • Refunds will only be processed after the session. No-shows and reschedule policy still applies to you, even if you are within this group.

If you want to declare yourself as a minority group, please check the box. I believe that people should trust each other, so I will not ask for proofs on your statement.
