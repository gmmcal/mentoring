Improve your development skills with 1-1 mentoring

Hello! My name is Gustavo Cunha. I have almost 20 years of experience developing all sorts of web-based software. I strongly believe that teaching is the best way to learn, so I want to dedicate some of my time to teach you something and learn something from you.

What we can talk about

In order for everything work well and we can learn together, we need to setup some ground rules on what you can expect from me.

  1. I'm highly specialized on backend development and have some knowledge on frontend and devops tools. I mostly know things about Ruby, Python and PHP. Since programming languages seems alike, I can understand a bit of some others.
  2. There is no such thing as a stupid question. Feel free to ask anything and I'll try my best to help you.
  3. I can try to code review your code. For that, please send a link for your repository/(pull|merge) request during your appointment and be clear that your want a code-review session. This link has to be publicly accessible.
  4. I can help you prepare for interviews. I've done this for companies already. I can't guarantee that you will succeed your job application (but I do hope you will).
  5. I can check your CV and give you tips on how to improve. Please, send a PDF link of your CV and we can talk about it. This link has to be publicly accessible.
  6. I can give you tips on next steps on your career and help you decrypt what the market is looking for.
  7. Don't expect I know everything. I'm here to help you, but also to learn from you.

What we can't talk about

  1. I will not pair program with you. The idea is to talk about development career, not write code along with you. Pair programming takes much more time and requires previous domain knowledge that I might not have enough.
  2. I will not review code that is intended to be submitted for a job application. This is not fair to other applicants. If I suspect this is the intention, I will stop the session.
  3. Due to ethical reasons, I will not review code that is held on private repositories nor owned by private companies, unless they are open sourced.

Commitment Fee

I've organized a few events on my life, and one thing I learned is that anything that is given for free lacks commitment from the consumer party. To avoid this problem, I decided to place what I call a Commitment Fee.

The Amount

The amount I charge for this is €10,00 (ten euros) for one session of 30 minutes for one person. Please, do not book multiple people for one session. This amount is non-refundable, in accordance to Refund Policy.

Refund Policy

  • I will not process refunds for no-shows.
  • If you can't attend a session, please let me know with at least 24 hours before your session. I will allow you to book a new time free of charge.
  • You can postpone 1 time per session payment. If you reschedule one session and can't make it, I will not reschedule nor refund.
  • If I need to postpone the session, you will be allowed to reschedule two sessions instead of one, with no extra costs.
  • You will probably receive an email from Calendly after your booking. Please, do not use the cancel nor reschedule links provided on the email (if any) without my express approval.
  • Any changes for this Refund Policy will be effective on the day the code is published on Github. If your booking was done before that, you will not be eligible for it, regardless if you benefit or not by the change. New policies do not apply for rescheduling.

Tech Minority Commitment

If you belong to any group that is part of a tech minority, like LGBTQA+, woman, people of color, low-income, people with any disabilities, etc, your fee will can refunded after the session, if you want, according to the following criteria:

  • I have 3 business days to confirm the refund.
  • I will try to refund you in full minus Wise Transfer Fees*. I will send you a proof of the transfer via email.
  • Whenever possible, I will try to absorb the fees and return 100% of the Commitment Fee. There is no guarantee that this will happen, unfortunately.
  • Refunds will only be processed after the session. No-shows and reschedule policy still applies to you, even if you are within this group.

If you want to declare yourself as a minority group, please check the box. I believe that people should trust each other, so I will not ask for proofs on your statement.

* Wise Transfers varies according to cross-border policies that I have no control.
